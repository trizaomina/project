By Admin | August 10 2023

Women's Empowerment Conference

She Africa hosted a two-day conference focused on women's empowerment, leadership, and entrepreneurship. The event featured keynote speakers, panel discussions, and interactive workshops covering topics such as gender equality, financial literacy, and career development. Participants had the opportunity to network with industry experts, mentors, and fellow attendees, gaining valuable insights and inspiration to pursue their goals.

By Admin | December 10 2023

Community Outreach Program:

As part of its commitment to community development, She Africa organized a community outreach program in partnership with local NGOs and community leaders. The event included health screenings, educational workshops, and skill-building activities aimed at empowering women and girls in underserved communities. Volunteers provided free healthcare services, distributed educational materials, and facilitated discussions on women's rights and social issues.

By Admin | March 10 2024

International Women's Day

She Africa celebrated International Women's Day with a series of events and activities honoring the achievements and contributions of women worldwide. The festivities included a cultural showcase featuring performances, art exhibitions, and storytelling sessions highlighting women's voices and experiences. Additionally, She Africa organized advocacy campaigns and fundraising initiatives to support women-led projects and initiatives.

By Admin |February 13 2024

Digital Skills Training Workshop

Recognizing the importance of digital literacy in today's digital economy, She Africa hosted a digital skills training workshop for women and girls. The workshop covered topics such as basic computer skills, internet navigation, and digital marketing, equipping participants with essential skills for success in the digital age. Trainers provided hands-on guidance and personalized support, empowering attendees to leverage technology for personal and professional growth.

By Admin | September 10 2023

Women in Leadership Forum

The forum provided a platform for women to share their experiences, insights, and best practices for overcoming barriers and advancing in leadership roles. Participants engaged in discussions on topics such as mentorship, networking, and inclusive leadership, exploring strategies to promote gender diversity and equality in leadership positions.

By Admin | December 20 2023

Girls' Education Symposium

The symposium featured discussions on girls' rights to education, strategies to increase enrollment and retention rates, and the importance of gender-responsive teaching methods. Speakers included education experts, policymakers, and representatives from NGOs working to improve girls' access to education. The event also showcased success stories of girls who have overcome obstacles to pursue their education and achieve their dreams
